Final Year Project

This project is a Unity-based choice-driven game exploring mental health themes through player agency, narrative design, and environmental storytelling.

The player takes on the role of a game developer trying to manage their daily life by repeatedly choosing between sleeping, working, and socialising. Over time, these choices affect the character’s wellbeing and cause the game world to change around them.

Role

Designer and developer

Tools

Unity, C#, Rider, Blender

Focus

Mental health representation, player choice, and environmental feedback
